## PART B: XCITIZEN DEPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

**xHansa Fellowship — 24-Month Deployment Agreement**

This agreement is entered into between **xCelero Labs** ("the League") and **____________________** ("the Xcitizen"), effective as of the Xcitizen's deployment start date.

### 1. TERM
The deployment term is **24 months** from the start date, ending at the cliff (Month 24). The stipend stops on the last day of Month 24. There is no grace period.

### 2. COMPENSATION
The Xcitizen will receive a **needs-based stipend** of $______/month (between $500 and $1,200, as determined by Strategic Command). This is not a salary. There are no benefits. The Xcitizen is a deployed operator, not an employee.

### 3. COMMITMENT
- The Xcitizen commits to **full-time deployment** for 24 months.
- The Xcitizen **may not** hold other employment, consulting, or significant external commitments during the deployment.
- **Early departure** (before Month 24) results in **total forfeiture** of all equity and performance grants earned to date.

### 4. THE GATE SYSTEM
The Xcitizen's Pod will be assessed at Gates 1, 2, 3, and 4 (Months 6, 12, 18, 24). Each Gate is binary: pass or fail.
- **Gate failure** triggers the **Kill Switch**: the Pod is dissolved within 48 hours.
- The Xcitizen may be **reassigned** (to another Pod, a support role) or **cycled out** (severed with pro-rated equity).
- **There is no appeals process.**

### 5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY — THE SUPREMACY CLAUSE
- All IP created during the deployment — inventions, code, designs, processes, data — **belongs to the League Commons**, not the individual Xcitizen.
- The Xcitizen **surrenders all individual IP claims** in exchange for access to the League Commons (the shared IP, infrastructure, and intelligence of the entire Hansa network).
- Breakthroughs are shared across the network: when one Pod solves a problem, every Pod benefits.

### 6. THE CLIFF (MONTH 24)
At Month 24, the deployment ends for all Xcitizens. Outcomes:
- **Keepers (top 3%):** Transition to permanent payroll within the spun-out venture entity. Salary, benefits, and equity acceleration apply.
- **The Cycled (remaining 97%):** Severed with **performance equity grants** carrying **36-month vesting schedules**. XCitizen status and network access retained. No salary.
- The determination of Keeper vs. Cycled is made by Strategic Command based on cumulative performance across all 4 Gates, Neural Link data, and Pod Leader assessment. The decision is final.

### 7. NEURAL LINK PROTOCOL
The Xcitizen agrees to wear the biometric monitoring device 24/7 during deployment. Data collected (heart rate variability, sleep patterns, stress indicators, cognitive load) is used to:
- Optimize Pod performance
- Flag performance degradation before the Pod is aware of it
- Inform Gate assessments and Keeper/Cycled determinations
Data is confidential to Strategic Command and the Pod's assigned medical/ops team. It is not shared with employers, external parties, or other Xcitizens.

### 8. CONFIDENTIALITY
The Xcitizen agrees to maintain confidentiality regarding:
- ProtoCo details (before public launch)
- Other Pods' operations and Gate results
- League Commons IP not yet publicly released
- Strategic Command deliberations

### 9. CODE OF CONDUCT
- Operate with **radical honesty** — report failures immediately; do not hide problems.
- **No hoarding** — share information, resources, and breakthroughs with the League Commons.
- **No politics** — the Gate system eliminates politics; do not reintroduce them.
- **Respect the Kill Switch** — if your Pod is dissolved, accept it and move to reassignment without negotiation.
- **No sabotage** — of other Pods, other Xcitizens, or the League. Immediate cycling out if confirmed.

### 10. ACKNOWLEDGMENT
By signing below, the Xcitizen acknowledges that they have read, understood, and accepted all terms of this agreement — including the 24-month commitment, the stipend (not salary), the Supremacy Clause (IP to the League), the Gate System (binary pass/fail), the Kill Switch (48-hour dissolution), and the Cliff (3% Keepers, 97% Cycled).

**The Xcitizen affirms:** _I am a deployed operator, not an employee. I build for the League, not for myself. I accept the Gate, the Kill Switch, and the Cliff. I am here because I choose to build infrastructure for the Global South, and this is the deal that lets me do it._
