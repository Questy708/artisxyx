# 28. CODE OF CONDUCT

**xHansa Fellowship — Xcitizen Code of Conduct**

---

**PARTIES:** This Code of Conduct binds all Xcitizens of the xHansa Fellowship, effective from the first day of the Crucible and continuing through the deployment and post-deployment (as an XCitizen alumnus).

---

## 1. THE PRINCIPLE

The xHansa Fellowship is a military-grade knowledge-deployment pipeline. It is not a workplace, a school, or a community program. It is an operational deployment. The Code of Conduct reflects this: it is strict, binary, and non-negotiable. Violations are not "coached" — they are adjudicated, and the consequences are immediate.

## 2. THE FIVE OBLIGATIONS

### Obligation 1: Radical Honesty

You will report the truth — about your Pod's progress, about your own performance, about failures and risks — **immediately and without spin.**

- Report failures the moment they happen. Hiding a failure is worse than the failure itself.
- Report risks before they materialize. If you see the train coming, say so.
- Do not sugarcoat. Do not hedge. Do not manage perceptions. The Gate System relies on accurate data; if you corrupt the data, you corrupt the system.
- If you don't know, say "I don't know." Inventing answers is a violation.

**Violation:** Withholding a material failure or risk from Strategic Command or your Pod Leader. Consequence: Warning (first) → Cycling out (second).

### Obligation 2: No Hoarding

You will share all information, resources, and breakthroughs with the League Commons. **When one Pod solves a problem, every Pod benefits.**

- Contribute learnings weekly to the Commons (failures included — especially failures).
- Share vendor contacts, supply chain solutions, regulatory workarounds, and technical breakthroughs.
- Do not withhold IP, even temporarily, for "competitive advantage" within the cohort.
- If your Pod discovers something that could help another Pod, you are obligated to share it — proactively, not upon request.

**Violation:** Withholding a breakthrough or resource from the League Commons. Consequence: Immediate cycling out.

### Obligation 3: No Politics

The Gate System eliminates politics by making every outcome binary and data-driven. **You will not reintroduce politics.**

- Do not lobby Strategic Command for favorable Gate thresholds.
- Do not form factions, alliances, or "coalitions" within the cohort.
- Do not negotiate, argue, or appeal a Gate determination. The data is the data.
- Do not undermine other Pods to make your Pod look better by comparison.
- Do not curry favor with Pod Leaders or Strategic Command through social means.

**Violation:** Attempting to influence a Gate determination through non-data means, or undermining another Pod. Consequence: Immediate cycling out.

### Obligation 4: Respect the Kill Switch

If your Pod is dissolved via the Kill Switch, you will **accept it and move to reassignment without negotiation.**

- You will not argue the determination.
- You will not rally your Pod to "appeal."
- You will not contact other Pods to generate sympathy or support.
- You will not badmouth the League, Strategic Command, or the Gate System (internally or externally) after a Kill Switch.
- You will cooperate with the asset transfer process (IP, code, hardware) within 48 hours.

**Violation:** Any of the above. Consequence: Immediate cycling out with equity forfeiture (the Kill Switch itself does not forfeit equity; fighting it does).

### Obligation 5: No Sabotage

You will not sabotage — directly or indirectly — other Pods, other Xcitizens, or the League.

- No deliberate interference with another Pod's operations, supply chain, or customer relationships.
- No spreading rumors or damaging information about other Xcitizens.
- no deliberate withholding of Commons contributions that you know another Pod urgently needs (this overlaps with Obligation 2, but is listed separately because the consequence is more severe).
- No actions designed to cause another Pod to fail a Gate.

**Violation:** Any act of sabotage. Consequence: Immediate cycling out with equity forfeiture and permanent loss of XCitizen status.

## 3. CONDUCT TOWARD FELLOW XCITIZENS

### 3.1 Respect and Dignity
- You will treat every Xcitizen with respect, regardless of their Archetype, background, education, or origin.
- The Hansa model selects on evidence of operating, not on credentials. An informal-economy operator and a PhD are equals here.
- Discrimination (on race, gender, religion, ethnicity, nationality, sexual orientation, disability, or age) is a violation. Consequence: Immediate cycling out.

### 3.2 No Harassment
- Sexual harassment, physical intimidation, verbal abuse, and bullying are strictly prohibited.
- The Crucible is intense by design, but intensity is not a license for abuse. Crucible instructors and Pod Leaders are held to the same standard.
- Report any harassment to the designated ombudsperson (independent of Strategic Command) within 48 hours. Reports are confidential. Retaliation against a reporter is a violation.

**Violation:** Harassment or retaliation. Consequence: Immediate cycling out, and referral to law enforcement where applicable.

### 3.3 No Romantic or Financial Entanglements Within a Pod
- Romantic or sexual relationships between members of the same Pod are prohibited during deployment (the power dynamics and operational risk are too high).
- Financial entanglements (loans, investments, joint ventures) between members of the same Pod are prohibited during deployment.
- Relationships with Xcitizens in **other** Pods are permitted but must be disclosed to Strategic Command (for conflict-of-interest management).

**Violation:** Undisclosed or prohibited entanglement. Consequence: One or both parties reassigned to different Pods; if reassignment is not possible, cycling out.

## 4. CONDUCT TOWARD THE LEAGUE AND THE COMMONS

### 4.1 Stewardship of League Resources
- You will use XEmbassy facilities, equipment, and materials responsibly and for deployment purposes only.
- You will not remove League property from XEmbassy premises without authorization.
- You will report damage, loss, or theft immediately.

### 4.2 Stewardship of League Commons IP
- You will contribute to the Commons actively and honestly (Obligation 2).
- You will not use Commons IP for personal purposes during or after deployment (per the IP Agreement).
- You will protect Commons IP from external disclosure (per the NDA).

### 4.3 Representation of the League
- You will not publicly represent the League (speak to media, post on social media as an official representative) without authorization.
- You will not use the Xcitizen title or League branding for personal gain or external ventures.
- You will refer all media inquiries to press@xcelero.com.

## 5. CONDUCT TOWARD EXTERNAL PARTIES

### 5.1 Anchor Partners and Customers
- You will treat Anchor Partners, customers, and vendors with professionalism and honesty.
- You will not make commitments on behalf of the League or any spun-out venture without authorization.
- You will not misrepresent the ProtoCo's capabilities, the Gate status, or the League's backing.

### 5.2 No Bribery or Corruption
- You will not offer, give, or accept bribes, kickbacks, or improper benefits — in any form, to anyone, in any jurisdiction.
- You will not facilitate bribery through third parties.
- If a partner or official demands a bribe, you will refuse, report it to Strategic Command immediately, and document the encounter.

**Violation:** Bribery or corruption. Consequence: Immediate cycling out, and referral to law enforcement.

### 5.3 No Conflicts of Interest
- You will disclose any existing relationship with an Anchor Partner, vendor, or customer that could create a conflict of interest.
- You will not deploy to a ProtoCo where a family member or close associate has a financial interest, without disclosure and Strategic Command approval.

## 6. PERSONAL CONDUCT

### 6.1 Substance Use
- You will not be under the influence of alcohol or non-prescription drugs during Crucible sessions, Pod operations, or external partner meetings.
- The Neural Link Protocol tracks physiological data; substance use that degrades performance will be flagged.
- Moderate alcohol use during off-hours is permitted. Abuse is not.

### 6.2 Physical and Mental Health
- You are responsible for maintaining the physical and mental fitness required for deployment.
- You will report any medical condition (physical or mental) that may affect your ability to deploy — promptly and honestly.
- The League provides emergency medical coverage and access to the medical/ops team. Use it.
- Mental health is not a weakness; hiding a mental health issue that degrades your deployment is.

### 6.3 Legal Compliance
- You will comply with all laws of your deployment jurisdiction.
- You will not engage in illegal activity, on or off duty, during deployment.

## 7. ADJUDICATION AND CONSEQUENCES

### 7.1 Process
- Alleged violations are reported to the **Conduct Officer** (independent of Strategic Command).
- The Conduct Officer investigates within 72 hours.
- The Xcitizen is informed of the allegation and given 24 hours to respond.
- The Conduct Officer issues a determination: No violation / Warning / Reassignment / Cycling out.
- For severe violations (sabotage, harassment, bribery, IP breach), the determination is escalated to Strategic Command for immediate action.

### 7.2 Consequences (Summary)

| Violation | Consequence |
|-----------|-------------|
| Withholding a failure/risk (first) | Warning |
| Withholding a failure/risk (second) | Cycling out |
| Withholding a Commons breakthrough | Cycling out |
| Lobbying/negotiating a Gate | Cycling out |
| Undermining another Pod | Cycling out |
| Fighting a Kill Switch | Cycling out + equity forfeiture |
| Sabotage | Cycling out + equity forfeiture + permanent ban |
| Discrimination | Cycling out |
| Harassment | Cycling out + law enforcement referral |
| Bribery/corruption | Cycling out + law enforcement referral |
| IP breach (NDA/Supremacy Clause) | Cycling out + equity forfeiture + legal action |

### 7.3 No Appeals
Conduct determinations, like Gate determinations, are final. The only exception is a **process error** (the investigation was flawed, new evidence emerges), which must be claimed within 7 days.

## 8. THE AFFIRMATION

By signing below, the Xcitizen affirms:

*I will operate with radical honesty. I will not hoard. I will not politic. I will respect the Kill Switch. I will not sabotage. I will treat every Xcitizen with dignity. I will steward the League's resources and IP. I will conduct myself with integrity toward all external parties. I accept that violations have immediate consequences, and that this Code is the operational foundation of the Hansa model.*
