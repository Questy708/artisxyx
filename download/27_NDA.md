# 27. NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT (NDA)

**xHansa Fellowship — Confidentiality Agreement**

---

**PARTIES:**
- **The League** (xCelero Labs, herein "the League")
- **The Xcitizen** ([Full Name], herein "the Xcitizen")

**EFFECTIVE DATE:** [Date]

---

## 1. PURPOSE

The Xcitizen will have access to highly confidential information during their deployment — ProtoCo details, other Pods' operations, League Commons IP, Strategic Command deliberations, and other Xcitizens' biometric data. This NDA governs the protection of that information.

## 2. DEFINITIONS

2.1 **"Confidential Information"** means any non-public information disclosed to or observed by the Xcitizen during the deployment, including but not limited to:

- **ProtoCo Confidential Information:** Commissioned venture concepts, Minimal Version designs, pilot data, customer data, Anchor Partner terms, unit economics, Gate metrics, the Playbook (before public release)
- **League Commons Confidential Information:** Shared IP not yet publicly released, operational templates, platform code, infrastructure designs, vendor lists, partner relationships
- **Pod Confidential Information:** Other Pods' operations, Gate results (before cohort-wide publication), internal Pod dynamics, Neural Link data of other Xcitizens
- **Strategic Command Confidential Information:** Gate criteria (field-specific thresholds), Pod formation algorithm details, Keeper/Cycled deliberations, financial models, fund structures, LP information
- **Xcitizen Personal Information:** Other Xcitizens' biometric data, assessment scores, medical information, personal circumstances
- **xCelero Corporate Confidential Information:** Business plans, financial information, investor information, employee information, trade secrets

2.2 **"Public Information"** means information that is:
- Already publicly available at the time of disclosure (through no breach by the Xcitizen)
- Independently developed by the Xcitizen without use of Confidential Information
- Rightfully received from a third party without breach of confidentiality
- Required to be disclosed by law or court order (with prior notice to the League where legally permitted)

## 3. OBLIGATIONS

3.1 The Xcitizen agrees to:

a) **Hold all Confidential Information in strict confidence** and not disclose it to any third party, during or after the deployment, for a period of **7 years** post-deployment (except for trade secrets, which are protected indefinitely).

b) **Use Confidential Information solely for the purpose of the deployment** — building the ProtoCo, contributing to the League Commons, and operating within the Pod. No personal use, no external projects, no side ventures.

c) **Protect Confidential Information** with at least the same degree of care used to protect their own most sensitive information, and in no case less than reasonable care.

d) **Not copy, reproduce, or distribute** Confidential Information except as necessary for the deployment. All copies remain League property.

e) **Return or destroy** all Confidential Information upon termination of deployment (at the League's direction). Digital materials on League devices are wiped; personal devices must be verified clean.

f) **Not use Confidential Information to compete** with the League or any spun-out venture, during deployment and for 24 months post-deployment (the "Non-Compete Period").

3.2 The Xcitizen specifically agrees regarding:

- **Neural Link data of other Xcitizens:** This is the most sensitive personal data in the program. The Xcitizen will not access, discuss, or reference any other Xcitizen's biometric data, even if inadvertently observed. Violation is grounds for immediate cycling out.

- **Gate results of other Pods:** The League publishes Gate results cohort-wide (transparency is a feature). However, the Xcitizen will not discuss other Pods' failures externally, in any context, ever. A Pod that failed is not a story for your LinkedIn.

- **ProtoCo details before launch:** The Xcitizen's own ProtoCo is confidential until the League authorizes public disclosure (typically at Demo Day or Gate 2). Until then, no external discussion.

## 4. PERMITTED DISCLOSURES

4.1 The Xcitizen may disclose Confidential Information to:
- Other members of their Pod (on a need-to-know basis)
- Strategic Command and Pod Leaders (as part of operations)
- League medical/ops team (for Neural Link health monitoring)
- Legal/financial advisors (under equivalent confidentiality)

4.2 The Xcitizen **may not** disclose Confidential Information to:
- Family members or friends
- Former employers or colleagues
- Media or journalists (refer all press inquiries to press@xcelero.com)
- Social media (no posts about Pod operations, other Xcitizens, Gate results, or ProtoCo details without League authorization)
- Future employers (during the Non-Compete Period, regarding League Confidential Information)

## 5. SOCIAL MEDIA AND PUBLIC STATEMENTS

5.1 The Xcitizen **may** publicly state:
- That they are an Xcitizen in the xHansa Fellowship (after the cohort is publicly announced)
- The Civilizational Field they are deployed in (e.g., "I'm deploying in Water Systems")
- General reflections on the experience (without disclosing Confidential Information)

5.2 The Xcitizen **may not** publicly state (without League authorization):
- ProtoCo details (the specific venture being built)
- Gate results (their own or others') before cohort-wide publication
- Other Xcitizens' assessment data, biometric data, or personal information
- League financial information, LP information, or Strategic Command deliberations
- Any information that could advantage a competitor or harm a spun-out venture

5.3 The League may, at its discretion, provide social media guidelines and pre-approved talking points during the deployment.

## 6. NON-COMPETE

6.1 During deployment and for **24 months** post-deployment, the Xcitizen agrees not to:
- Found, join, or advise a venture that directly competes with their ProtoCo or any other active League ProtoCo in the same Civilizational Field
- Use League Confidential Information to develop a competing product or service
- Solicit League Anchor Partners, vendors, or LPs for competing ventures

6.2 This non-compete is narrowly scoped to the Xcitizen's deployed Field and ProtoCo. The Xcitizen is free to work in other fields or in non-competing ventures.

6.3 The Xcitizen acknowledges that this non-compete is reasonable given the access to League Commons IP and the nature of the deployment.

## 7. NON-SOLICITATION

7.1 During deployment and for 12 months post-deployment, the Xcitizen agrees not to:
- Solicit other Xcitizens to leave the League for external ventures
- Solicit League employees, contractors, or partners for external ventures

7.2 Nothing in this section prevents Xcitizens from collaborating on post-deployment ventures through the League (e.g., a Cycled Xcitizen joining a Keeper's spun-out entity is permitted if the entity is part of the League ecosystem).

## 8. TERM AND SURVIVAL

8.1 This NDA is effective from the Effective Date and continues for the deployment term.

8.2 Confidentiality obligations survive for **7 years** post-deployment (except trade secrets: indefinite).

8.3 Non-compete obligations survive for **24 months** post-deployment.

8.4 Non-solicitation obligations survive for **12 months** post-deployment.

## 9. BREACH AND REMEDIES

9.1 Breach of this NDA results in:
- Immediate cycling out (forfeit of all equity)
- Injunctive relief (the League may seek a court order to prevent further disclosure)
- Damages (the League may seek monetary compensation for harm caused by the breach)
- Permanent loss of XCitizen status and Commons access

9.2 The Xcitizen acknowledges that monetary damages alone may be inadequate for a confidentiality breach, and that injunctive relief is appropriate.

## 10. RETURN OF MATERIALS

10.1 Upon termination of deployment (for any reason), the Xcitizen will:
- Return all League devices (Neural Link wearable, laptop if provided, access badges)
- Return or destroy all physical materials (documents, prototypes, hardware)
- Submit personal devices for verification that no Confidential Information remains
- Confirm in writing that all materials have been returned or destroyed
