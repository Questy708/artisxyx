# 25. FELLOWSHIP AGREEMENT — xHansa Deployment

**xHansa Fellowship — 24-Month Deployment Agreement**

---

**PARTIES:**
- **The League** (xCelero Labs, herein "the League" or "xCelero"), a [jurisdiction] entity with offices at [address]
- **The Xcitizen** ([Full Name], herein "the Xcitizen"), an individual residing at [address]

**EFFECTIVE DATE:** [Date — the Crucible start date]

---

## 1. TERM

1.1 The deployment term is **24 months** from the Effective Date, ending on [Cliff Date — Month 24].

1.2 The deployment is divided into five phases:
- Phase 0: The Crucible (8 weeks)
- Phase 1: Q1 — 0-to-1 Pilot (Months 1–6)
- Phase 2: Q2 — First Revenue (Months 7–12)
- Phase 3: Q3 — Expansion (Months 13–18)
- Phase 4: Q4 — Institutionalization (Months 19–24)

1.3 The stipend terminates on the last day of Month 24. There is no grace period, no extension, and no severance beyond the performance equity described in Section 6.

## 2. NATURE OF RELATIONSHIP

2.1 The Xcitizen is a **deployed operator**, not an employee. This Agreement does not create an employment relationship, and no employment rights are conferred or implied.

2.2 The Xcitizen is not entitled to:
- Salary, wages, or compensation beyond the stipend (Section 3)
- Employee benefits (health insurance beyond emergency medical, dental, vision, retirement, paid leave, vacation, or severance)
- A guarantee of continued deployment beyond the current phase (Section 4 — Gates)
- A guarantee of employment after Month 24 (Section 6 — The Cliff)

2.3 The Xcitizen **is** entitled to:
- The stipend (Section 3)
- Housing, workspace, equipment, and travel as described in the Offer Letter
- Performance equity, determined at the cliff (Section 6)
- XCitizen status and network access (retained for life, post-deployment)

## 3. COMPENSATION — STIPEND

3.1 The League will pay the Xcitizen a **needs-based stipend** of $[amount]/month, payable on the 1st of each month.

3.2 The stipend amount is determined by Strategic Command based on:
- Deployment location (cost of living)
- Dependent status (declared in the onboarding form)
- Operational needs of the ProtoCo

3.3 The stipend is **tied to operational outcomes**, not time served. If the Xcitizen's Pod is dissolved (Kill Switch, Section 5), the stipend continues for a maximum of 14 days while the Xcitizen is reassigned. If the Xcitizen is cycled out, the stipend terminates on the date of cycling.

3.4 The stipend is reviewed at Month 6 and Month 12 for needs changes (e.g., new dependents, relocation). Upward adjustments are not guaranteed.

3.5 The stipend is **not a salary**. It is an operational allowance. No taxes are withheld by the League. The Xcitizen is responsible for their own tax obligations in their jurisdiction of residence.

## 4. THE GATE SYSTEM

4.1 The Xcitizen's Pod will be assessed at five Gates:
- **Gate 0** (end of Crucible, Week 8): Crucible certification
- **Gate 1** (Month 6): Minimal Version deployed + Anchor Partner LOI
- **Gate 2** (Month 12): Revenue + sustainable unit economics
- **Gate 3** (Month 18): Replication in 3+ geographies + Playbook delivered
- **Gate 4** (Month 24): Local operator transition + 80%+ operational efficiency

4.2 Each Gate is **binary: pass or fail**. There is no subjective evaluation, no committee vote, no partial credit. Either the Pod hits the published thresholds or it does not.

4.3 Gate criteria are field-specific and disclosed to each Pod at commissioning. The criteria are set by Strategic Command and are not negotiable.

4.4 **There is no appeals process.** Gate determinations are final. The data is the data.

## 5. THE KILL SWITCH

5.1 If a Pod fails Gate 1, 2, or 3, the **Kill Switch** is triggered.

5.2 Upon Kill Switch activation:
- The Pod is dissolved within **48 hours** of the Gate determination.
- Assets (IP, code, hardware, vendor relationships) are transferred to the League Commons.
- Pod members are individually assessed for reassignment.

5.3 Xcitizen reassignment outcomes:
- **Reassignment to another Pod** (if a compatible slot exists)
- **Reassignment to a support role** (XEmbassy operations, League Commons, Strategic Command)
- **Cycling out** (severed with pro-rated performance equity, Section 6.3)

5.4 The Xcitizen acknowledges that the Kill Switch may end their deployment before Month 24. This is an inherent risk of the program, accepted at signing.

## 6. THE CLIFF — MONTH 24

6.1 At Month 24, the deployment ends for all Xcitizens, regardless of Gate 4 outcome. The stipend stops.

6.2 **Keepers (top 3%):** The highest-performing Xcitizens across the cohort, as determined by Strategic Command, transition to permanent payroll within the spun-out venture entity. Terms of that employment are separate from this Agreement.

6.3 **The Cycled (remaining 97%):** Severed with a **performance equity grant** in the spun-out entity (if Gate 4 passed) or no equity (if Gate 4 failed), carrying a **36-month vesting schedule** (12-month cliff, monthly vesting thereafter).

6.4 The Keeper/Cycled determination is made by Strategic Command based on:
- Cumulative Gate performance across all four quarterly Gates
- Neural Link biometric and cognitive data
- Pod Leader assessment
- Contribution to the League Commons

6.5 The determination is **final**. There is no appeal.

## 7. COMMITMENT AND EXCLUSIVITY

7.1 The Xcitizen commits to **full-time deployment** for the 24-month term.

7.2 The Xcitizen **may not** engage in:
- Other employment (full-time or part-time)
- Consulting or advisory work for external parties
- Founding or operating a separate venture
- Any activity that materially conflicts with the deployment

7.3 **Early departure** (voluntary withdrawal before Month 24) results in:
- **Total forfeiture** of all equity and performance grants earned to date
- Immediate cessation of the stipend
- Loss of XCitizen deployable status (network access retained)

7.4 The Xcitizen may request a leave of absence (medical, family emergency) in writing to Strategic Command. Leaves are granted at Strategic Command's discretion, are unpaid (stipend pauses), and do not extend the cliff date. A leave exceeding 30 days may result in cycling out.

## 8. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY — THE SUPREMACY CLAUSE

*(Detailed in the separate IP Agreement — Document 26. This section is a summary; the IP Agreement controls.)*

8.1 All IP created during the deployment belongs to the **League Commons**, not the individual Xcitizen.

8.2 The Xcitizen surrenders all individual IP claims in exchange for access to the League Commons.

8.3 Breakthroughs are shared across the network. When one Pod solves a problem, every Pod benefits.

## 9. NEURAL LINK PROTOCOL

9.1 The Xcitizen agrees to wear the biometric monitoring device **24/7** during deployment (excluding charging time and medical exceptions).

9.2 Data collected:
- Heart rate variability (HRV)
- Sleep patterns
- Stress indicators (galvanic skin response)
- Cognitive load proxies (activity patterns, response times)

9.3 Data usage:
- Optimizing Pod performance (identifying burnout risk before it cascades)
- Flagging performance degradation for preemptive intervention
- Informing Gate assessments and Keeper/Cycled determinations
- Medical monitoring (during the Crucible specifically)

9.4 Data confidentiality:
- Data is accessible to Strategic Command, the Pod's assigned medical/ops team, and the Xcitizen themselves.
- Data is **not** shared with other Xcitizens, other Pods, external employers, or any third party without the Xcitizen's written consent.
- Aggregate, anonymized data may be used for research and algorithm improvement.

9.5 The Xcitizen may request access to their own data at any time.

## 10. CONFIDENTIALITY

*(Detailed in the separate NDA — Document 27. This section is a summary; the NDA controls.)*

10.1 The Xcitizen agrees to maintain confidentiality regarding:
- ProtoCo details (before public launch)
- Other Pods' operations and Gate results
- League Commons IP not yet publicly released
- Strategic Command deliberations
- Other Xcitizens' biometric or assessment data

10.2 Confidentiality obligations survive the termination of this Agreement.

## 11. CODE OF CONDUCT

*(Detailed in Document 28. This section is a summary; the Code of Conduct controls.)*

11.1 The Xcitizen agrees to:
- Operate with **radical honesty** — report failures immediately; do not hide problems
- **No hoarding** — share information, resources, and breakthroughs with the League Commons
- **No politics** — the Gate system eliminates politics; do not reintroduce them
- **Respect the Kill Switch** — accept dissolution without negotiation
- **No sabotage** — of other Pods, Xcitizens, or the League

11.2 Violations may result in immediate cycling out (forfeit of equity).

## 12. TERMINATION

12.1 **By the League:**
- Kill Switch (Section 5) — Pod-level, binary
- Code of Conduct violation (Section 11) — individual, immediate
- Medical incapacity (certified by the medical team) — individual, with pro-rated equity

12.2 **By the Xcitizen:**
- Voluntary withdrawal (Section 7.3) — total equity forfeiture

12.3 Upon termination:
- Stipend ceases per the terms above
- Housing, workspace, and equipment access revoked within 7 days
- IP remains with the League Commons (Section 8)
- NDA and confidentiality obligations survive (Section 10)
- XCitizen status and TownSquare access retained (unless terminated for Code of Conduct violation)

## 13. GOVERNING LAW AND DISPUTE RESOLUTION

13.1 This Agreement is governed by the laws of [jurisdiction — Mauritius or the XEmbassy country].

13.2 Disputes are resolved through binding arbitration in [jurisdiction], conducted in English. The Xcitizen waives the right to litigation in court.

13.3 The Xcitizen acknowledges that the Gate System, the Kill Switch, and the Keeper/Cycled determination are **not subject to dispute resolution**. They are binary operational decisions, not legal disputes. Only process errors (e.g., the Gate was assessed with incorrect data) are subject to review, and only within 7 days of the determination.

## 14. ACKNOWLEDGMENT

By signing below, the Xcitizen acknowledges that they have:
- Read this Agreement in full
- Had the opportunity to seek independent legal advice
- Understood all terms, including the 24-month commitment, the stipend (not salary), the Supremacy Clause (IP to the League), the Gate System (binary), the Kill Switch (48-hour dissolution), and the Cliff (3% Keepers, 97% Cycled)
- Accepted these terms freely and without coercion

**The Xcitizen affirms:**
*I am a deployed operator, not an employee. I build for the League, not for myself. I accept the Gate, the Kill Switch, and the Cliff. I am here because I choose to build infrastructure for the Global South, and this is the deal that lets me do it.*
