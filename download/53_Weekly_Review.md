---

## PURPOSE

The Weekly Review is the Pod's Friday checkpoint. It assesses the week's sprint, updates the Gate trajectory, surfaces risks, and sets next week's direction. It is mandatory, time-boxed (60 min), and documented.

The Comms fills in this template during the meeting and posts it to the Pod Slack channel by end of day Friday.

## WEEKLY REVIEW RULES

1. **Every Friday.** No skipping. Even if the sprint goal wasn't achieved (especially then).
2. **60 minutes.** Time-boxed. If you need more, schedule a separate meeting.
3. **Everyone reports.** No exceptions. If a member has nothing to report, that's data (why are they idle?).
4. **The Comms documents.** The notes are posted to the Pod channel by end of day. The Pod Leader reads them before Monday.
5. **Action items have owners and due dates.** Vague actions ("we should look into X") are not action items. Specific tasks with specific owners and specific dates are.
6. **Honesty over performance.** If the sprint failed, say so. If the Gate is at risk, say so. Hiding problems is worse than the problems.
