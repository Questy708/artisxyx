---

## PURPOSE

The ProtoCo Dossier is the master document for your commissioned venture. It defines what you're building, why, for whom, and how it maps to the Gate system. It is the first document your Pod creates (in the Crucible's Reconstruction phase) and the reference point for every decision through Month 24.

**Start this in Week 6 of the Crucible.** Complete it by the end of Week 7. The Pod Leader reviews it before deployment begins.

## DOSSIER RULES

1. **The Commission Statement (Section 1) is immutable** unless Strategic Command approves a change. You cannot change what you were asked to build.
2. **The Solution (Section 3) evolves.** Update it as you learn. The Minimal Version definition may adjust slightly, but the core must hold.
3. **The Gate Mapping (Section 6) is your strategic compass.** Review it monthly. If the evidence column is blank, you're not on track.
4. **The Dossier is a living document.** Update it at every Monthly Review. Version it (v1.0, v1.1, etc.).
5. **The Pod Leader reviews every version.** Strategic Command reviews it at each Gate.
