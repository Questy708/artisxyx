Before You Begin
The xHansa Fellowship is a 24-month human capital deployment engine. It is not an educational program, an accelerator, or a job. It is a military-grade knowledge-deployment pipeline. Read this page carefully. Answer honestly. There are no right answers — only real ones. We observe behavior, not stated preference.

Before you begin, you must understand:

• This is a 24-month commitment with no exit option before the cliff.

• The stipend is $500–$1,200/month, needs-based. There is no salary. No benefits.

• Individual IP is subordinate to the League Commons (the Supremacy Clause).

• Quarterly Gates are binary: pass or fail. Failure triggers the Kill Switch (Pod dissolution within 48 hours).

• Month 24 is a hard cliff. The top 3% ("Keepers") transition to permanent payroll. The remaining 97% ("The Cycled") are severed with performance equity (36-month vesting).

If any of these terms are unacceptable, this program is not for you.

Selection Process
1
Application review (within 21 days) — We read every application. You'll hear back regardless of outcome.
2
Civilizational Field Typing Assessment (if shortlisted) — A behavioral assessment, not an interview. We observe how you operate under pressure.
3
Deployment Classification — Based on the assessment, you're matched to a field and an Archetype.
4
Pod Formation — A deterministic algorithm composes your 10-person Pod to maximize complementary strengths.
5
The Crucible (8 weeks) — Onboarding. Neural reprogramming. If you survive, you deploy.
6
24-Month Deployment — Q1 Pilot → Q2 Revenue → Q3 Expansion → Q4 Institutionalization → The Cliff.

Selection criteria (in order): Evidence of operating > Acceptance of commitment terms > Field alignment and deployable thinking > Self-awareness under pressure.

We do not select on credentials, university, or work history. We select on the evidence that you operate — and that you operate for something larger than yourself.

The Six Archetypes
Pods are composed of six Archetypes: Pilot, Builder, Hustler, Operator, Tracker, and Comms. Your Archetype is determined by observed behavior under stress during the Crucible — not by what you pick here. But we want to know how you see yourself.
Pilot
I set direction. I see the path before others do.
Builder
I construct. I turn ideas into working things.
Hustler
I close. I get the deal, the partner, the customer.
Operator
I maintain. I keep the system running when others are spent.
Tracker
I surveil. I find the data, the signal, the risk before it hits.
Comms
I translate. I make the complex legible to the people who need it.
