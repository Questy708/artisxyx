## 1. PURPOSE

This Agreement governs all intellectual property ("IP") created by the Xcitizen during their 24-month deployment in the xHansa Fellowship. It implements the **Supremacy Clause** of the Hansa model: individual IP is subordinate to the League Commons.

## 2. DEFINITIONS

2.1 **"IP"** means all intellectual property of any kind, including but not limited to:
- Inventions, discoveries, and improvements (whether or not patentable)
- Software, code, algorithms, and architectures
- Designs, schematics, and technical drawings
- Processes, methods, and operational procedures
- Data, datasets, and databases
- The Playbook (the codified operational manual for a ProtoCo)
- Written materials, documentation, and training materials
- Trade secrets and know-how
- Brand assets created for the ProtoCo (names, logos, marks)

2.2 **"League Commons"** means the shared IP pool of the Hansa network — all IP contributed by all Pods across all cohorts, accessible to all Xcitizens and ventures within the League.

2.3 **"Deployment IP"** means all IP created by the Xcitizen during the deployment term (Section 3), whether created individually, jointly with the Pod, or in collaboration with other Pods.

2.4 **"Background IP"** means IP the Xcitizen created or owned before the deployment, which is not subject to this Agreement.

2.5 **"ProtoCo"** means the commissioned venture concept assigned to the Xcitizen's Pod.

## 3. SCOPE — WHAT BELONGS TO THE LEAGUE

3.1 **All Deployment IP belongs to the League Commons.** This is absolute and non-negotiable.

3.2 Deployment IP includes anything created:
- During the Crucible (Phase 0)
- During any deployment phase (Phases 1–4)
- Using League resources (XEmbassy facilities, equipment, League Commons IP, Pod collaboration)
- Related to the ProtoCo, even if conceived outside working hours or off-site
- Related to any other ProtoCo, Field, or xCelero venture the Xcitizen encounters during deployment

3.3 The Xcitizen **assigns** all right, title, and interest in all Deployment IP to the League, effective upon creation. No further assignment is required.

## 4. THE SUPREMACY CLAUSE

4.1 **Individual interests are superseded by the League.** When one Pod discovers a solution, every Pod benefits. When one Stack cracks a supply chain problem in Lagos, every Stack in Accra, Nairobi, and Cairo benefits immediately.

4.2 The Xcitizen **may not**:
- Claim individual ownership of any Deployment IP
- File patents or trademarks in their own name for Deployment IP
- Use Deployment IP for personal ventures (during or after deployment) without League license
- Withhold or hoard IP from the League Commons
- Share Deployment IP with external parties without League authorization

4.3 The Xcitizen **may**:
- Access the entire League Commons for the duration of their deployment
- Continue accessing the League Commons post-deployment (as an XCitizen alumnus), subject to the confidentiality terms of the NDA
- Use Background IP (pre-deployment creations) that they brought to the League
- Cite their deployment work in their professional portfolio (without disclosing confidential details)

## 5. LEAGUE COMMONS — ACCESS AND CONTRIBUTION

5.1 **Access:** The Xcitizen has full access to the League Commons IP repository during deployment. This includes:
- IP from all prior cohorts
- IP from other active Pods (real-time contributions)
- xCelero's platform code, infrastructure designs, and operational templates
- Anchor Partner networks and vendor relationships

5.2 **Contribution:** The Xcitizen must contribute to the League Commons:
- All Deployment IP (automatic — see Section 3)
- Documented learnings, failures, and breakthroughs (submitted weekly to the Commons)
- The Playbook (at Gate 3, the codified operational manual becomes League Commons IP)
- Any process improvements or tools developed during deployment

5.3 **Attribution:** The League may, at its discretion, attribute contributions to the Xcitizen by name in the Commons. Attribution is a recognition, not a property right.

## 6. BACKGROUND IP

6.1 The Xcitizen must disclose all Background IP (pre-deployment IP) that may be relevant to the ProtoCo, in writing, at the start of deployment. Use the Background IP Disclosure Form (attached as Schedule A).

6.2 Background IP remains the property of the Xcitizen. However, if Background IP is incorporated into Deployment IP or the ProtoCo, the Xcitizen grants the League a **perpetual, irrevocable, royalty-free, worldwide license** to use that Background IP within the League Commons.

6.3 Failure to disclose relevant Background IP may result in the League treating it as Deployment IP (i.e., League-owned).

## 7. POST-DEPLOYMENT IP RIGHTS

7.1 After deployment (the cliff at Month 24), the Xcitizen:
- **Retains** XCitizen status and League Commons access (read-only, subject to NDA)
- **Does not retain** any ownership of Deployment IP
- **May not** use Deployment IP to found a competing venture without League license
- **May** use the skills, experience, and network gained during deployment for any future work (skill and experience are not IP)

7.2 If the Xcitizen is a **Keeper** (top 3%, transitions to the spun-out entity), they continue creating IP for that entity under separate employment terms — but the entity's IP flows back to the League Commons per the venture's agreement with the League.

7.3 If the Xcitizen is **Cycled** (97%), they carry performance equity in the spun-out entity but no IP rights. They are free to build something new — just not with the Deployment IP.

## 8. MORAL RIGHTS

8.1 The Xcitizen waives all moral rights (rights of attribution, integrity) in Deployment IP to the extent permitted by law.

8.2 The League will make reasonable efforts to attribute Xcitizen contributions in the Commons (Section 5.3), but this is a courtesy, not a legal obligation.

## 9. PROTECTION AND ENFORCEMENT

9.1 The League, not the Xcitizen, decides whether to patent, trademark, or otherwise protect Deployment IP.

9.2 The Xcitizen agrees to sign all documents and take all actions necessary to perfect the League's ownership of Deployment IP (e.g., patent assignments, trademark applications).

9.3 The Xcitizen agrees to promptly disclose all Deployment IP to the League (via the weekly Commons submission process) so the League can evaluate protection.

## 10. BREACH

10.1 Breach of this Agreement (e.g., withholding IP, filing a personal patent on Deployment IP, sharing Deployment IP externally) results in:
- Immediate cycling out (forfeit of all equity)
- Legal action to recover or enjoin misused IP
- Permanent loss of XCitizen status and Commons access

10.2 The Xcitizen acknowledges that IP is the League's most valuable asset. Breaches are treated as the most serious violation of the Hansa model.

## 11. TERM AND SURVIVAL

11.1 This Agreement is effective for the deployment term (24 months) and **survives indefinitely** thereafter with respect to:
- IP ownership (Deployment IP remains League property forever)
- Confidentiality (per the NDA)
- Post-deployment restrictions (Section 7)

11.2 The League Commons license to Background IP (Section 6.2) is perpetual and irrevocable.
