## APPLICATION INSTRUCTIONS

The xHansa Fellowship is a 24-month human capital deployment engine. It is not an educational program, an accelerator, or a job. It is a military-grade knowledge-deployment pipeline. Read this form carefully. Answer honestly. There are no right answers — only real ones. We observe behavior, not stated preference.

**Before you begin:**
- 20–30 minutes
- A quiet space (some questions require you to think under pressure)
- (Optional) Links to anything you've built or operated

**What you'll need to understand before applying:**
- This is a 24-month commitment with no exit option before the cliff.
- The stipend is $500–$1,200/month, needs-based. There is no salary. There are no benefits.
- Individual IP is subordinate to the League Commons.
- Quarterly Gates are binary: pass or fail. Failure triggers the Kill Switch (Pod dissolution within 48 hours).
- Month 24 is a hard cliff. The top 3% transition to permanent payroll. The remaining 97% are severed with performance equity (36-month vesting).

If any of these terms are unacceptable, this program is not for you.

## WHAT HAPPENS NEXT

1. **Application review** (2–3 weeks): We read every application. You'll hear back within 21 days.
2. **Civilizational Field Typing Assessment** (if shortlisted): A behavioral assessment — not an interview. We observe how you operate under pressure.
3. **Deployment Classification**: Based on the assessment, you're matched to a field and an Archetype.
4. **Pod Formation**: A deterministic algorithm composes your 10-person Pod to maximize complementary strengths.
5. **The Crucible** (8 weeks): Onboarding. Neural reprogramming. If you survive, you deploy.
6. **24-Month Deployment**: Q1 Pilot → Q2 Revenue → Q3 Expansion → Q4 Institutionalization → The Cliff.

**Selection criteria (in order):**
1. Evidence of operating (Section 2)
2. Acceptance of the commitment terms (Section 3)
3. Field alignment and deployable thinking (Section 4)
4. Self-awareness under pressure (Sections 5–6)

We do not select on credentials, university, or work history. We select on the evidence that you operate — and that you operate for something larger than yourself.
