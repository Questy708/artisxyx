## WHAT HAPPENS NEXT

1. **Application review** (rolling, 1–2 weeks): We read every application. You'll hear back within 14 days.
2. **Interview** (30–45 min, video): If your application resonates, we'll talk. This is a conversation, not an interrogation.
3. **Decision** (within 7 days of interview): Yes, no, or waitlist.
4. **Onboarding** (if accepted): Pre-program orientation, logistics, travel.

**Selection criteria (in order):**
1. Evidence of building (Section 2)
2. Alignment with the collective model (Section 3)
3. Domain obsession (Section 4)
4. Self-awareness and honesty (Section 5)

We do not select on credentials, university, or work history. We select on the evidence that you build, and that you build for something larger than yourself.
