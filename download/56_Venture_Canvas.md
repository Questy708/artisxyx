## PURPOSE

The Venture Canvas is a one-page strategic overview of your ProtoCo. It captures the essential elements of the venture on a single page — the problem, solution, users, economics, and Gate mapping. It is derived from the ProtoCo Dossier (Document 55) and is the most-referenced document in the Pod's daily operations.

**Create it in Crucible Week 7.** Print it. Pin it to your Pod workspace wall. Update it at every Monthly Review.

## HOW TO USE THE CANVAS

### Creation (Crucible Week 7)
The Pod fills out the canvas together during the Reconstruction phase. The Pilot facilitates. The Comms documents. Every member contributes from their Archetype's perspective:
- **Pilot:** Sections 1, 2, 9 (problem, strategy, Gate trajectory)
- **Builders:** Section 4, 7 (solution, Minimal Version)
- **Hustler:** Sections 5, 6 (Anchor Partner, unit economics)
- **Operators:** Section 6, 7 (unit economics, Minimal Version deployability)
- **Tracker:** Sections 8, 10 (competitive advantage, risks)
- **Comms:** Section 11 + documents the whole canvas

### Display
Print the canvas on A3 or larger paper. Pin it to the Pod workspace wall. It should be visible to every member at all times. Also keep a digital version in Notion.

### Weekly Reference
In the Weekly Review (Friday), the Pilot references the canvas:
- "Are we on track for the Gate?" → Check Section 9.
- "What's the sprint goal?" → Derived from Sections 7 (Gate 1), 6 (Gate 2), etc.
- "What risks are we tracking?" → Check Section 10.

### Monthly Update
At the Monthly Review, update:
- Section 6 (unit economics) with actual data
- Section 9 (Gate trajectory) with current status
- Section 10 (risks) with the current top 3
- Version number and date

### Gate Submission
The Venture Canvas is part of the Gate submission evidence. It shows Strategic Command that the Pod has a clear, documented strategy.

---

## CANVAS RULES

1. **One page.** If it doesn't fit, it's too detailed. The canvas is the overview — the ProtoCo Dossier has the detail.
2. **Concise.** Each section is 1-3 sentences or bullet points. Not paragraphs.
3. **Current.** Update it monthly. A stale canvas is worse than no canvas.
4. **Shared.** Every member can see it, reference it, and suggest updates.
5. **The Pilot owns it.** The Pilot is responsible for keeping it current and accurate.
