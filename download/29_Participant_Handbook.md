## 1. WELCOME

You're reading this because you passed Gate 0 and you're about to deploy. This handbook is your operational reference for the next 24 months. Keep it on your device. Refer to it often. When in doubt, it has the answer — or tells you who to ask.

This is not a welcome letter. It is a manual. Read it.

## 2. WHAT YOU'VE JOINED

The xHansa Fellowship is a **24-month human capital deployment engine**. You are an Xcitizen — a deployed operator, not an employee, not a student, not a fellow in the traditional sense. You will build infrastructure for the Global South across one of Nine Civilizational Fields, as part of a 10-person Pod, under a binary Gate system, ending in a cliff where 3% of you transition to permanent roles and 97% are cycled out with equity.

This is not a defect. It is the design. The League builds ventures, not careers.

## 4. THE FIVE PHASES

### Phase 0: The Crucible (8 Weeks)
An 8-week psychological demolition and reconstruction. "The Shock" (systematic destabilization of habitual thought patterns) and "The Wall" (sustained cognitive exhaustion). You emerge with an execution-first cognitive architecture. If you don't complete the Crucible, you don't deploy.

### Phase 1: Q1 — 0-to-1 Pilot (Months 1–6)
Your Pod builds the Minimal Version — not a slide deck, not a business plan, but a functioning asset deployed in the field. You secure an Anchor Partner LOI (non-negotiable). At Month 6, Gate 1: did you deploy? Did you get the LOI? If not, the Kill Switch.

### Phase 2: Q2 — First Revenue (Months 7–12)
Monetize. Every metric — CAC, LTV, churn, throughput — must hit precise thresholds. Revenue is the sole proof of product-market fit. No substitutes. At Month 12, Gate 2: are the numbers there? If not, the Kill Switch.

### Phase 3: Q3 — Expansion (Months 13–18)
Replicate the pilot in 3–5 new geographies with different constraints. Author the permanent Playbook — a codified operational manual any future operator can execute. At Month 18, Gate 3: did it transfer? Is the Playbook real? If not, the Kill Switch.

### Phase 4: Q4 — Institutionalization (Months 19–24)
Replace yourself. Recruit and train local permanent operators. Operational efficiency must exceed 80% under local control. At Month 24, Gate 4 — and the cliff. The stipend stops. Keepers (3%) transition to payroll. The Cycled (97%) are severed with equity.

## 5. THE NINE CIVILIZATIONAL FIELDS

Every ProtoCo is commissioned against one of these fields:

1. **Water Systems** — atmospheric generation, desalination, purification, distribution
2. **Energy Systems** — microgrids, generation, storage, distribution
3. **Food & Agriculture** — vertical farms, supply chains, crop science, post-harvest
4. **Health & Mobility** — diagnostics, cold-chain, last-mile delivery, therapeutics
5. **Built Environment** — modular construction, housing, urban systems
6. **Data & Intelligence** — connectivity, compute, AI, mesh networks
7. **Logistics & Trade** — cross-border, freight, payments, market access
8. **Governance & Identity** — digital identity, land registry, civic tech
9. **Education & Human Capital** — learning platforms, maker spaces, skills

Your field assignment was determined by the Typing Assessment and the commissioning process. You expressed preferences; the needs of the commission came first.

## 6. THE SIX ARCHETYPES

Every Pod has all six:

| Archetype | Role |
|-----------|------|
| **Pilot** | Sets direction. Sees the path before others. |
| **Builder** | Constructs. Turns ideas into working things. |
| **Hustler** | Closes. Gets the deal, the partner, the customer. |
| **Operator** | Maintains. Keeps the system running when others are spent. |
| **Tracker** | Surveils. Finds the data, the signal, the risk before it hits. |
| **Comms** | Translates. Makes the complex legible to the people who need it. |

Your Archetype was determined by observed behavior under stress during the Crucible — not by what you picked on your application. It is confirmed, not chosen. If you disagree with your assignment, the Crucible data is the evidence. Argue with the data, not with Strategic Command.

## 7. THE GATE SYSTEM

Five Gates. Binary pass/fail. No subjective evaluation. No committee votes. No appeals.

| Gate | Timing | Pass Criterion |
|------|--------|----------------|
| Gate 0 | End of Crucible (Week 8) | Crucible certification (attendance, behavior, Archetype stability, recovery) |
| Gate 1 | Month 6 | Minimal Version deployed 30+ days + Anchor Partner LOI signed |
| Gate 2 | Month 12 | Revenue at/above field threshold + unit economics met (CAC < LTV/3, LTV > 3×CAC, churn < 10%) |
| Gate 3 | Month 18 | 3+ geographies deployed + Playbook delivered and tested + replication economics met |
| Gate 4 | Month 24 | Local operators running independently + 80%+ efficiency on all KPIs |

Gate results are published cohort-wide within 24 hours. Transparency is a feature.

## 8. THE KILL SWITCH

If your Pod fails Gate 1, 2, or 3: the Pod is dissolved within 48 hours. Assets transfer to the League Commons. You are individually reassigned (to another Pod, a support role) or cycled out.

There is no appeals process. The speed is intentional — lingering failure corrodes morale and wastes capital.

## 9. THE SUPREMACY CLAUSE (IP)

All IP you create during deployment belongs to the League Commons. Individual interests are superseded by the League. When one Pod solves a problem, every Pod benefits.

You don't own what you build. You share in the upside through equity. You gain access to the entire Commons in exchange. This is the deal. It is non-negotiable.

## 10. THE CLIFF — KEEPERS AND THE CYCLED

At Month 24, the deployment ends for everyone. The stipend stops. No grace period.

- **Keepers (top 3%):** Transition to permanent payroll in the spun-out entity. Salary, benefits, equity acceleration.
- **The Cycled (97%):** Severed with performance equity grants (36-month vesting). XCitizen status and network access retained.

This is not a failure. It is the design. The League builds ventures, not careers. The 97% carry equity, network, and operational experience into whatever they build next.

## 11. THE NEURAL LINK PROTOCOL

You will wear a biometric monitoring device 24/7 during deployment. It tracks:
- Heart rate variability (HRV)
- Sleep patterns
- Stress indicators
- Cognitive load proxies

This data is used to optimize Pod performance, flag burnout risk before it cascades, and inform Gate assessments. It is confidential — accessible to Strategic Command, your medical/ops team, and you. Not shared with other Xcitizens or external parties.

You can request your data at any time.

## 12. YOUR STIPEND

$[500–1,200]/month, needs-based, paid on the 1st of each month. This is not a salary. No benefits. No taxes withheld (you are responsible for your own tax obligations).

The stipend is reviewed at Month 6 and Month 12 for needs changes (dependents, location). Upward adjustments are not guaranteed.

If your Pod is dissolved (Kill Switch), the stipend continues for up to 14 days while you are reassigned. If you are cycled out, the stipend terminates on the date of cycling.

## 13. HOUSING, WORKSPACE, AND TRAVEL

- **Housing:** Shared housing at/near the XEmbassy during the Crucible. Field housing arranged during deployment phases.
- **Workspace:** Full access to XEmbassy facilities (foundry, lab, studio, commons) relevant to your ProtoCo.
- **Equipment:** Neural Link device, prototyping materials, tools, and equipment as needed.
- **Travel:** All deployment-related travel covered by the League (flights, ground transport, visas).

You are responsible for your personal effects, food (beyond what's provided at the XEmb commons), and any personal travel.

You must contribute weekly:

Attribution is a courtesy, not a property right. The Commons belongs to the League.

## 17. CODE OF CONDUCT — SUMMARY

Five obligations:
1. **Radical honesty** — report failures immediately; no spin
2. **No hoarding** — share everything with the Commons
3. **No politics** — the Gate eliminates politics; don't reintroduce them
4. **Respect the Kill Switch** — accept dissolution without negotiation
5. **No sabotage** — of other Pods, Xcitizens, or the League

Violations are adjudicated by the Conduct Officer (independent of Strategic Command). Severe violations = immediate cycling out. See Document 28 for the full Code.

## 18. CONFIDENTIALITY — WHAT YOU CAN AND CAN'T SAY

**You CAN say:**
- "I'm an Xcitizen in the xHansa Fellowship"
- The Civilizational Field you're deployed in
- General reflections (without disclosing specifics)

**You CANNOT say (without authorization):**
- ProtoCo details (the specific venture)
- Gate results (before cohort-wide publication)
- Other Xcitizens' assessment or biometric data
- League financial or Strategic Command information
- Anything that could advantage a competitor or harm a spun-out venture

See Document 27 (NDA) for the full confidentiality terms. Confidentiality survives for 7 years post-deployment.

## 19. HEALTH AND SAFETY

- **Emergency medical:** Covered by the League during deployment. Contact the Hub Manager or medical team immediately.
- **Mental health:** The Crucible and deployment are intense. The medical/ops team monitors Neural Link data for burnout risk. You can also self-refer. Mental health issues are not weaknesses; hiding them is.
- **Physical safety:** XEmbassy facilities have safety protocols. Follow them. Report hazards immediately.
- **The Crucible:** Intense by design, but monitored. If you feel physically unsafe, tell a Crucible instructor immediately. The Wall is hard; it is not dangerous.

## 20. WHAT TO DO IF THINGS GO WRONG

| Situation | What to do |
|-----------|-----------|
| Your Pod is failing a Gate | Report it to your Pod Leader immediately (radical honesty). Do not hide it. |
| You're burning out | Tell your Pod Leader or the medical team. Neural Link will likely flag it first, but self-reporting is faster. |
| You have a conflict with a Pod member | Try to resolve it within the Pod first. If it persists, escalate to the Pod Leader. If it's a Conduct violation, go to the Conduct Officer. |
| You witness a Conduct violation | Report to the Conduct Officer within 48 hours. Reports are confidential. Retaliation is prohibited. |
| You're considering leaving early | Talk to Strategic Command before deciding. Early departure = total equity forfeiture. Make sure you understand what you're giving up. |
| You have a medical emergency | Contact the Hub Manager or medical team immediately. For life-threatening emergencies, call local emergency services first, then notify the League. |
| You lose your Neural Link device | Report to the Hub Manager immediately. A replacement will be issued. Do not go more than 24 hours without it. |
| You're contacted by media | Refer all inquiries to press@xcelero.com. Do not comment. |
