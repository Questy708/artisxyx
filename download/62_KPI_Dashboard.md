## PURPOSE

The KPI Dashboard is the Pod's real-time performance display — the numbers that tell you whether you're on track for the next Gate. It is maintained by the Tracker, updated weekly, and displayed prominently in the Pod workspace and Notion.

If a KPI is trending below threshold, that's a Gate risk. The Dashboard makes it visible before it becomes a Kill Switch.

---

## DASHBOARD STRUCTURE

The Dashboard has four sections — one per phase. Only the current phase's KPIs are actively monitored, but all are visible (to show trajectory).

## DASHBOARD RULES

### 1. Updated Weekly
The Tracker updates the Dashboard every Friday before the Weekly Review. It is the first thing the Pod looks at in the review.

### 2. Honest Numbers
If MRR is $0, the Dashboard shows $0. If uptime is 72%, it shows 72%. Inflated numbers will be exposed at the Gate — and the Kill Switch doesn't care about your dashboard.

### 3. Visible to All
The Dashboard is displayed in:
- The Pod workspace (printed, on the wall)
- Notion (digital, accessible to all members + Pod Leader)
- The venture tracker (accessible to Strategic Command)

### 4. Trend Matters More Than Snapshot
A single data point is noise. A trend is signal. The Dashboard shows trend arrows (↑ improving, ↓ declining, → stable) for each KPI. Three consecutive weeks of ↓ on any Gate-relevant KPI triggers a Pod Leader intervention.

### 5. Gate-Relevant KPIs Are Non-Negotiable
The KPIs that map directly to Gate criteria (marked with 🟢/🟡/🔴) are the ones that matter most. If any is 🔴, the Pod is at risk of failing the Gate. The Pilot must address it immediately.

---

## THE TRAFFIC LIGHT SYSTEM

Each Gate-relevant KPI has a traffic light:

| Light | Meaning | Action |
|-------|---------|--------|
| 🟢 Green | On track or ahead | Continue |
| 🟡 Yellow | Slightly behind or at risk | Address in Weekly Review. Pilot adjusts. |
| 🔴 Red | Significantly behind or failing | Immediate Pod Leader escalation. Recovery plan required. |

**Three or more 🔴 KPIs = Strategic Command notification.** The Pod is in crisis.

---

## GATE READINESS SCORE

At any point, the Pod can calculate a "Gate Readiness Score" — the percentage of current-phase KPIs that are 🟢 or 🟡:

```
Gate Readiness = (🟢 + 🟡 KPIs) / (Total Gate-relevant KPIs) × 100%

> 80% = Likely to pass
60–80% = At risk — needs intervention
< 60% = Unlikely to pass — immediate escalation
```

The Tracker calculates this weekly and presents it in the Weekly Review. If it drops below 80%, the Pilot must present a recovery plan. If it drops below 60%, the Pod Leader escalates to Strategic Command.
