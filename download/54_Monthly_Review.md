# 54. MONTHLY REVIEW

**xHansa Fellowship — Pod Monthly Review Template**
**Pod [ID] | Month [X] of 24 | [Date]**

---

## PURPOSE

The Monthly Review is the Pod's deep strategic checkpoint — a 90-minute meeting on the last Friday of each month with the Pod Leader. It goes beyond the weekly sprint to assess Gate trajectory at the phase level, budget burn, risk posture, team health, and Commons engagement.

The Comms fills in this template during the meeting and posts a summary to the Pod channel. The Pod Leader submits the full review to Strategic Command.

## MONTHLY REVIEW RULES

1. **Last Friday of every month.** No exceptions.
2. **90 minutes.** Time-boxed. Every section gets its time.
3. **Pod Leader attends and leads Sections 4 and 8.** The Pod Leader's perspective is essential.
4. **The Comms documents.** Full notes posted to Pod channel within 24 hours.
5. **Strategic Command receives a copy.** The Pod Leader submits within 48 hours.
6. **Honesty is paramount.** If the Gate is Red, say Red. If the budget is blown, say it. If the team is burning out, say it. Hiding problems from Strategic Command is a Conduct violation (radical honesty).
7. **Action items are tracked.** The Comms follows up on action items in the Weekly Reviews until they're closed.
