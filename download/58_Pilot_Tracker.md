## PURPOSE

The Pilot Tracker monitors the deployment and operation of your Minimal Version in the field — from first deployment (Month 5) through the 30-day field operation period (Gate 1 evidence). It is the operational log of the pilot and a Gate 1 evidence document.

The Operator maintains this tracker. The Tracker reviews it weekly.

**Daily Log Rules:**

## TRACKER RULES

1. **The Operator owns it.** They log daily. The Tracker reviews weekly.
2. **Log in real-time.** Don't batch — memory degrades. Log issues the day they happen.
3. **Honest data.** If uptime is 70%, log 70%. Don't round up. The Gate assessment uses this data — inflated numbers will be exposed.
4. **Commons contributions.** Pilot learnings are among the most valuable Commons contributions. Submit them weekly.
5. **Gate evidence.** This tracker IS the Gate 1 evidence. It must be complete, accurate, and compiled by Month 6, Week 4.
